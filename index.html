Our Story

Where heritage
meets
the avant-garde

Founded in the 12th arrondissement of Paris, Maison Lumière emerged from a singular conviction: that in an age of the mass-produced, the rarest luxury is an object made entirely by one pair of hands.

Each piece moves through the atelier for a minimum of sixty hours before it finds its owner. We work with three family workshops — in Grasse, Florence, and Zurich — whose combined heritage spans four centuries.

Our Pillars

Uncompromising Quality

We reject volume in favour of virtue. Every item produced meets a standard defined not by market expectation, but by the ideal of the object itself.

Intimate Relationships

We know our clients by name. Each relationship is personal, discreet, and enduring. Your patronage is a conversation, not a transaction.
